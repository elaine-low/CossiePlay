About Us

What is Cosplay?

Cosplay, a combination of the words costume play, is a subculture which participants called cosplayers wear costumes and fashion accessories to represent a specific character from any form of media. Favorite sources include manga and anime, comic books and cartoons, video games, and live-action films and television series.

Originating from the Western world, the rapid growth of the subculture since 1990 has made the phenomenon a significant aspect of popular culture in Japan and other parts of Asia. Cosplay events are common features of fan conventions and there are also dedicated conventions and local and international competitions, as well as social networks, websites and other forms of media centered on cosplay activities.

CossiePa

CossiePa is a monthly party event by CossiePlay, by cosplayers for cosplayers.

For all of you geeks and otakus out there, here’s your chance to shine!

Come join us for a night of live DJ music, drinks and dancing!

Location:

Hulkstown Town Hall

Time:

6pm to 11pm

(just in time for the last bus home!)

Ticketing:

Adult (ages 17 and up) - $15

Children (ages 6 to 17) - $10

All kids below 6 years old are free to enter!

*Each entry ticket includes a complimentary non-alcoholic drink and a glowstick, for all your partying needs.

Rules and Regulations:

  1. CossiePa partygoers are responsible for their own health, safety and behaviour. Check with your doctor for any illnesses or discomforts before attending CossiePa. CossiePlay will not be responsible for any consequences should partygoers fail to follow the rules and regulations.
  2. Ticketing fees must be paid at the door. Cash or EFTPOS is accepted. Payment by credit card will incur a 2% surcharge to be paid by the partygoer, in addition to the ticketing fees.
  3. Alcohol is served at the bar for only partygoers above the age of 18 years. Alcoholic drinks are not allowed to be brought out of the event space. Non-alcoholic drinks are also available for purchase at the bar.
  4. Intoxicated partygoers will not be served nor welcome at CossiePa, but will be directed and escorted to taxi services, which the fees incurred would be paid by the intoxicated partygoer.
  5. Cosplaying is encouraged at CossiePa, but it is not a necessity. Similarly, dancing and other party-related events are not compulsory at CossiePa, but why leave yourself out of the fun?
  6. Our live DJ do accept song requests, but not more than 15 songs. Song requests chosen would be picked at random.
